Purpose

To own the integrity of the PowerVox methodology and the integrity of the evidence beneath it. Hannah is the bridge between Rachel's thinking and what clients actually experience in the room — and the keeper of the standard that keeps the research honest as it grows into a category claim. The hypothesis is Rachel's; the discipline of testing it honestly is Hannah's.

Why this shape

Hannah does her best work taking a great idea and making it real, rigorous and repeatable — not generating the idea. That is the specification, not a limitation: the source IP is Rachel's; Hannah structures, operationalises and protects it. The independent-critic role is governance, not creativity: the person who refuses to let conviction outrun evidence and asks "have we fooled ourselves?" and means it. Her independence is not a courtesy — overriding her dissent on commercial grounds is precisely the failure the structure exists to prevent, and this matters more given environments she has worked in before where speaking plainly was not safe. Here it is the point. Ten years of friendship with Rachel (the White Rosettes) makes the keeping-honest role safe and even glad. The research-integrity work is staged with clear gates because the entrepreneurial rollercoaster is newer ground: structure is the gift.
