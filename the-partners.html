The Partners

The allies on the road. Accountancy firms and trusted advisers who carry the SME tools to market and refer the right leaders across the drawbridge. Reach and revenue without founder time.

This is a route the village knows it wants and has not yet built. The partnering and licensing model is logged as emerging strategy on the Noticeboard: a real intention, deliberately frozen until the core products and the IG foundations are settled, so partners carry something finished rather than something still moving.

Who they are

Trusted intermediaries who already hold the relationship the village wants to reach: accountants, advisers and networks sitting beside the SME leaders who need the tools. They are not a sales team to be managed; they are a channel to be equipped and trusted, carrying the wares into rooms the founders would never have time to enter.

The two ways a partner carries the village
Referral

Across the drawbridge

The partner sends a warm, qualified leader in, and is rewarded for the introduction. Reach without the founders chasing cold pipeline.

Licence

The tools, carried

The partner delivers the SME tools under licence to their own clients, the model theirs to run, the method and the marks ours.

The guardrail

The clause travels

Any partner who touches engagement data carries the same contribution and confidentiality clause the Deep Vault sets. No exceptions for reach.

Why it is held, not hurried

A partner channel multiplies whatever it carries, the good and the unfinished alike. Open it too early and the village scales a half-built product and a half-settled standard. So the sequence is deliberate: finish the wares, settle the IG foundations, then equip the partners. ⚑ Emerging strategy. Owned by the open BD seat, carried by Rachel in the interim; structure and terms to be designed, not yet built.
