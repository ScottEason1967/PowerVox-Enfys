PP-07The Conditions Taxonomydrafted · v0.1 schema

The second instrument: a fixed schema of twelve dimensions in four groups (where the power sits, what the room receives, the capable and unheard, readiness to change the room) along which rooms differ. Fixed headings, variable values. The schema is the IP that compounds; the values are the client's, elicited fresh. Because the schema is fixed, every engagement deposits a comparable row into the pattern library. Widened only with a stated reason, since every new dimension is a cost paid by every future map. Set out on the Instrument.

PP-08 v0.1 draft · where the IP stops and the engagement begins

The room's rule. A position paper is reopened by its own named triggers, not by mood. Drafts in this room are written by the Cartographer from the founders' rulings and carry no authority until ratified — the drafting is faithful, the pen is theirs. The canonical language governs every word; where a paper conflicts with it, the language wins and the paper is corrected.
